### Transformers: Revenge of the Fallen

Transformers: *Revenge of the Fallen* is an American science fiction action film directed by **Michael Bay** and produced by **Steven Spielberg**. Released in **2009**, it is the sequel to *Transformers (2007)* and continues the war between the heroic **Autobots** and the evil **Decepticons** on Earth.

The movie follows Sam Witwicky and his allies as they join the Autobots to stop the Decepticons from destroying the world. The film features intense battle scenes, stunning special effects, and the iconic leadership of **Optimus Prime**.
